User Guide

Welcome to the Chesterton Building Management System! This dashboard gives you a live overview of key systems in the building. Here's a quick guide to help you get started:

  • **Search Bar:** Use the search bar at the top to quickly find systems by their name or location.
  • **Filters:** On the left side, you can filter the displayed systems by their status (Active, Alert, Inactive), location, or type. You can select multiple options to refine your search.
  • **System Cards:** Each card shows a system's name, location, current value, and its operational status with a color-coded badge.
  • **Admin Panel:** Click the "Admin Panel" button at the top to reveal a form where you can add new IoT systems to the dashboard.
